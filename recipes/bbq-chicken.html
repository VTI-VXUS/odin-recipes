BBQ Chicken Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • extra virgin olive oil
  • kosher salt to taste
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup of your favorite BBQ sauce

Instructions

  1. Get grill to high heat (450Â°F)
  2. Drizzle chicken breasts with olive oil and rub in
  3. Season with kosher salt and ground black pepper
  4. Place chicken on hot grill, cover and cook for 5-6 minutes
  5. Flip and cook for 4-5 minutes longer
  6. Baste chicken with BBQ sauce, cook for 2 minutes on each side
  7. Remove from grill and let rest for 5 minutes
